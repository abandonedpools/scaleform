 >>> HERE IS HOW YOU USE THE HUD. DO NOT MESSAGE ME ON DISCORD ASKING FOR HELP ABOUT THIS. <<<

 1. Place "panorama.my.zip" in C:/Users/yourname/AppData/roaming/HLAE. Do not extract the file, just drag & drop it there.

 2. Place the "p_scaleform" folder in your MIGI addons folder and click "UPDATE BUILD".

 3. Open the HLAE Custom Loader and launch CSGO with the commands "-game migi/csgo -afxdetourpanorama". (like this: https://prnt.sc/1--dbYV_INAd)

 (IF YOU GET AN ERROR WHEN LAUNCHING, MAKE SURE MIGI AND HLAE ARE UPDATED TO THE LATEST VERSION.)

 Download HLAE: https://github.com/advancedfx/advancedfx/releases

 Download MIGI: https://discord.gg/UmE9JG7 (ZooL's Server)

 -- You can customize certain aspects of the hud in p_scaleform\panorama\styles\csgostyles.css (bg opacity, hide panels, animations, etc.)

 -- To use the 2013 MVP panel (https://prnt.sc/FjRT16d_vp0x) go to p_scaleform\panorama\layout\hud\hudwinpanel.xml and change hudwinpanel.css to hudwinpanel1.css in line 4 (LIKE THIS: https://prnt.sc/JtaIMEUw-fAy) then REBUILD MIGI.
	
 -- To disable colored weapon names (cl_loadout_colorweaponnames 1), comment out everything from line 354 to 385 in panorama/styles/hud/hudweaponselection.css (LIKE THIS: https://prnt.sc/vizgldSzRzVJ) then REBUILD MIGI.
